Operation School Bell® 2019

Assistance League® of Colorado Springs is a nonprofit organization that helps students and families in El Paso County.  This year, we will again be partnering with Kohl’s at 3110 N. Powers Blvd to provide school clothing for students in kindergarten through twelfth grades.

Please try to arrive at your scheduled time, as you will not be able to shop if you arrive after 5:00 p.m. (weekdays) or 10:30 a.m. (Saturdays) on your scheduled day. If you are unable to shop on your scheduled day, please contact your school counselor to reschedule.

  • There will be a yellow plastic figure at the north door where you will enter and register. Only students who have a schoolbell passport signed by a parent or guardian will be served. Siblings will not be served unless their school has provided a Passport for them.
  • Students shopping must wear the wristband given to them to designate their money allotment.
  • Only school clothing may be purchased for students.  No hats, jewelry, or purses may be purchased.
  • Schoolbell-specific cash registers will be at the south end of the store; you may only check out with these designated cashiers.  You may not use any coupons for your School Bell purchases or receive Kohl’s Cash. If you are under your allotted amount, you may only return to the shopping floor one time to add items to your purchase.  If your purchase exceeds the designated amount, it will be your decision whether to pay any extra amount, up to $20.00, or not purchase all the clothing selected.
  • You will need to shop carefully as no items can be exchanged.  You will not receive a receipt for the purchase.

Kohl’s employees will not be able to answer specific questions concerning Operation School Bell. You may call the store on Sep 9-12, 16-19, 23-26, and Oct 1-3 between 3:00 and 5:00 p.m. or Sep 14, 21, 28 between  8:00 and 10:00 a.m. to talk to a volunteer from Assistance League.

 

Operation School Bell clothing is purchased by the Assistance League of Colorado Springs.
